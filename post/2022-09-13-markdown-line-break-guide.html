Markdown Line Break Guide

Date published: 2022-09-13

No Line Break

Hello(<-- No Spaces)
World

Result:
Hello World

Line Break (using Two Spaces)

Hello  (<-- Two Spaces)
World

Result:
Hello
World

[OPTIONAL] Line Break (using br tags)

Hello <br>
World

Result:
Hello
World

[NOT] a Line Break

Hello

World

Result:
Hello

World
